PERSON OF THE WEEK

Stefan Baumgartner is a web developer based in Linz, Austria. Committed to performance and making the web a faster place, he is part of the UX team at Dynatrace. Stefan co-hosts the Working Draft podcast and organizes events for the tech community, especially in his hometown. You can find him blogging and speaking about performance, web/design ops, progressive enhancement, and tooling at conferences and meetups around the world. In 2017, he published his first book Front-End Tooling with Gulp, Bower, and Yeoman . Thank you for your contributions to the community, dear Stefan!
